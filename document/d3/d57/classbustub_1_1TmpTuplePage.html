详细描述

TmpTuplePage format:

Sizes are in bytes. | PageId (4) | LSN (4) | FreeSpace (4) | (free space) | TupleSize2 | TupleData2 | TupleSize1 | TupleData1 |

We choose this format because DeserializeExpression expects to read Size followed by Data.
